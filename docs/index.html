Welcome to DrapeIt.me

Tired of shopping online and wondering how clothes will actually look on you?

Stop the guesswork! DrapeIt.me lets you virtually try on clothes and see how they fit before you buy.

How it Works:

  • Setup your profile by uploading your pictures.
  • Add the clothes you love from our selection and give it DrapeIt.
  • See yourself wearing them!
  • If you like your look, ShareIt ! BuyIt !

It's fast, fun, and takes the stress out of online shopping.
